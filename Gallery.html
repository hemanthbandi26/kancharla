KANCHARLA FOUNDATION

Founded by Dr. Sreekanth C. Kancharla (BTech, MBA, Senior Member IEEE), who is a rural entrepreneur,Udyam Awardee, and environmental advocate

Overview

Kancharla Foundation is a non‑governmental society based in Kandukur, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh. It was first registered on April 29, 2010 and later incorporated as a society on May 30, 2017 under the Udyam MSME program (Udyam‑AP‑13‑0003374)
Its stated mission is to support education, healthcare, sustainable agriculture, and community welfare, especially among socioeconomically disadvantaged populations.
