Overview

Kancharla Foundation is committed to creating a better future for underprivileged communities.

With a focus on education, healthcare, and sustainable living, we ensure that every donation makes a tangible impact. Our team works tirelessly to bring positive change and uplift those in need. From providing scholarships to children, organizing medical camps, to promoting organic farming, our initiatives are designed to empower individuals and foster self-sufficiency. We believe in the power of community-driven solutions and are dedicated to making a lasting difference, one life at a time.

Our Mission

Our Vision To eradicate poverty and uplift rural communities by empowering individuals through education, healthcare, and sustainable practices.
Food Security: Regular food drives for underserved communities.
Education: Scholarships and learning programs for children and youth.
Healthcare: Organizing medical camps and providing essential health services.
Sustainability: Promoting organic farming and environmental conservation.
